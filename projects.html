AskBuzz

AskBuzz was a project completed by me and a partner for Georgia Tech's yearly hackathon, HackGT. AskBuzz is a chat bot that allows students on Georgia Tech's website to get the answers to their questions about Georgia Tech without having to browse through webpage after webpage. The bot is named after our school mascot Buzz and we coded Buzz using Java.
